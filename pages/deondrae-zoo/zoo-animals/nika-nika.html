Nika Nika No Mi

Nika-Nika, also known as the Sun God Nika, is a divine entity in the One Piece world. This celestial figure represents the sun's radiance and power, bestowing light and vitality upon the world. In the series, the Nika-Nika no Mi enables its user to harness the Sun God's abilities, potentially controlling light and energy with unparalleled might.
