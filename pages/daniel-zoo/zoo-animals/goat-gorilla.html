About the Goat Gorilla

The goat gorilla is a large animal which bears a similar build to the gorilla, while exhibiting physical attributes akin to a goat. It is generally uniform in terms of coloration, apart from small sections of its body which appear in different pigmentations. Goat gorillas are only known to have either a murky white or a dark red hide, with brown features appearing in both cases. The goat gorilla's face is relatively intimidating, composed of large green eyes, a striking nose, and a mouth with two tusks protruding upward from the mandible. Two ivory horns extend from the creature's head, with two large ears dangling on either side, and a single tuft of brown fur grows from the center of its back. Most wild goat gorillas appear dark red in coloration.
