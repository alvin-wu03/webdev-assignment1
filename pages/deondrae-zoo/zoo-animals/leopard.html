Neko Neko no Mi, Model: Leopard

Leopards, found across Africa and parts of Asia, are renowned for their striking appearance, characterized by golden fur adorned with rosette patterns. These elusive big cats are highly adaptable, skilled climbers, and formidable hunters, making them top-tier predators in the animal kingdom. In the world of One Piece, Rob Lucci, empowered by the Leopard Zoan fruit, mirrors the leopard's prowess with extraordinary abilities.
